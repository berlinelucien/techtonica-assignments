Mom's Awesome Spaghetti

By using some science and a bit of elbow grease, my spaghetti and meat sauce recipe gets the most flavor out of the ingredients in the least amount of time.

Previous Next

Ingredients:

    2 Tbs. olive oil

    1 green bell pepper, chopped

    1 small onion, chopped

    1 jar of spaghetti sauce

    1 lb. ground turkey

    1 box of spaghetti noodles

Procedure:

Make the spaghetti sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over the medium-high heat.
  2. Once the ould is hot, add the green bell pepper and onion, and sautee until they are soft.
  3. Add the ground turkey to the pan and pan-fry until it is cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.
  4. Once the turkey is cooked through, pour in the jar of spaghetti sauce. Reduce the heat to low, stir to combine, and let simmer.

    5. Make the spaghetti noodles

    1. Heat a large pot of water on high heat, until boiling.
    2. Once boiling, add the box of spaghetti noodles and cook following the package's directions (usually 9-12 minutes).
